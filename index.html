      How To Play

      Build one 4-letter, 5-letter, 6-letter, and 7-letter word each round using the three highlighted reel letters. Letters can repeat inside your word.

      Word values: 4 = 100, 5 = 200, 6 = 350, 7 = 500. Every word score is multiplied by your Survival multiplier.

      Clear a full round for +400, get +250 for solving in 4-5-6-7 order, and gain +10 for every second left. Survival increases after a cleared round and resets if time runs out or you skip.
