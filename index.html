How To Play

Rules

Use the three highlighted wheel letters to find one 4-letter, 5-letter, 6-letter, and 7-letter word each round. Letters can repeat inside your word.

Word values: 4 = 100, 5 = 200, 6 = 350, 7 = 500. Every word score is multiplied by Survival.

Clear the full round for +400, earn +250 for solving in 4-5-6-7 order, and gain +10 for every second left on the timer.

In multiplayer, both players share the same board. Ready up, race through eight rounds, and the higher score wins.
